Our services

Qualitative

Also known as primary research, qualitative analysis demands interaction and real-time data. This is the process of collating data that hasn’t been out there yet. Interviews, market research surveys, questionnaires, and more fall under qualitative or primary research. That's it for now.

Quantitative

When numbers and figures come together to form a proper analysis, it is called quantitative research. It is majorly used to find patterns and averages, make predictions, and generalize results to a wider population.Quantitative research is widely used in understanding customer psychology.
